Freshly Cooked Chicken Bacon Pasta

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bacon
  • Pasta
  • Tomato paste
  • Thickened Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cut up bacon and add to pan on low heat
  2. Cut up chicken and add to pan
  3. Add pasta to pot and boil
  4. Add spices to chicken
  5. Once Chicken has been throughly cooked
  6. Add pasta to pan
  7. Add cream to pan and mix
  8. Add paste to pan and mix
  9. Add Parmesan cheese to pan and mix
  10. Leave to cook through for 10 mins while slowly stiring
